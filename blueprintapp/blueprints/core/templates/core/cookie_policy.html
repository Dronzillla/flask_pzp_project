Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our site.

2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use only one type of cookie:

  • Essential Session Cookie: This cookie is generated by Flask-Login to maintain your session and is necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Remember Me Functionality: If you select the "Remember Me" option during login, a persistent session cookie will be stored on your device. This allows you to stay logged in even after closing your browser. Cookie will remain valid for 365 days after last interaction with a site.
4. Cookie Consent

When you visit our website, we display a cookie consent popup. By clicking "Accept", you consent to the use of our session cookie as described in this policy.

5. Managing Cookies

Our website requires the session cookie to function. Therefore, if you do not wish to accept cookies, please do not use our website or disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
